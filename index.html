Quant · On-chain 2026 Python · CLI

polytrader

"From Polymarket data toolkit to a self-running quant desk."

A Python system that crawls all Polymarket markets, runs walk-forward backtests, and places live limit orders against the V2 CLOB. Backtest gating is structural — execute refuses to send orders if win rate, Sharpe, or profit factor don't pass thresholds. Hard caps on per-trade and total exposure round out the safety harness.
